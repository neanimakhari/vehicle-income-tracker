# Vendor Management Policy

## Purpose
Assess and manage third-party risk.

## Scope
All third-party services that handle platform or tenant data.

## Policy
- Perform due diligence before onboarding vendors.
- Maintain a vendor inventory with data classification and access scope.
- Require security questionnaires and data processing agreements (DPAs).
- Review vendor security posture annually.

## Monitoring
- Track vendor incidents and CVEs impacting the platform.
- Re-assess vendors after major service changes or breaches.
