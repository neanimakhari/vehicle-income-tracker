# Incident Response Policy (Template)

## Purpose

Define incident identification, response, and communication procedures.

## Severity levels

- P1: System outage or data exposure
- P2: Partial outage or degraded performance
- P3: Minor issue, no impact on data security

## Response steps

- Detect and classify incident
- Contain, eradicate, recover
- Perform post-incident review within 5 business days
